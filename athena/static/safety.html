TRAVEL SAFETY GUIDLINE

Before your travel
    Make sure you are in the best health condition
    Make sure your passport is valid for at least another 6 months
   Check the current covid-19 situation at your designation on Safeflights
During your travel
    Avoid large crowds or crowded areas.
    Avoid contact with sick people, especially if they have a cough, fever or difficulty breathing.
    Wash your hands often with soap under warm running water for at least 20 seconds.
    Use alcohol-based hand sanitizer if soap and water are not available. Always keep some with you when you travel.
Planning for your trips
    Avoid designations with high risk of covid rating on Safeflights
    Check with your travel insurance provider to find out if you are covered for medical treatment if you become infected with COVID-19.
Keep in touch
Stay in contact with your family and friends in your home town through emails, letters, phone calls, video chats or social media. Tell them about your experiences and keep them up to date on your whereabouts and any changes to your travel plans.
