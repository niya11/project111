OUR MISSION

In 2019, our live change all of the sudden due to the spread of covid 19. The virus stops us from doing our most favorable activities from meeting friends, working, studying to what most of us looking forward to do in every holiday: Travelling. However, after a year fighting agaisnt the covid-19, we start to see the light at the end of the tunnel. We wish that one day, we can once again pack our luggages and head to the beaches.

Safeflights’s mission is to help you plan for your next vacation while being transparent with the current covid-19 situation at you desired designation. We wish you can enjoy your trip while keep youself and your family safe from covid-19.
